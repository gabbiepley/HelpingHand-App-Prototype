Welcome to HelpingHand App!

Our mission at HH is to make it easier for people to connect with movements in their communities that they are passionate about.

We simply facilitate a link between a volunteer's interests and local non-profit organizations that are categorized by those interests, and then make it easy to keep track of your completed service hours.

To sign up or login, first choose whether you are an organization or a volunteer.
